As a Designer

Abby Brooks, the creative mind behind RooksRoom Graphics, who is making a name for herself in the graphic design world with her unique blend of bold creativity and thoughtful approach. With a fascination for the raw, unapologetic aesthetic of brutalism, Abby's designs often incorporate elements of this style, but she's not afraid to explore and adapt to a wide range of visual languages. From editorial and magazine design to branding and visual identity, Abby's portfolio is a testament to her versatility and willingness to take on new challenges. With a curious and open-minded approach, she's always seeking out new inspirations and ways to grow as a designer. Abby's dedication to her craft and her passion for creative expression make RooksRoom Graphics a exciting and dynamic presence in the design world.
